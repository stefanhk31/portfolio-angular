About Me

I am passionate about web development. I love the creativity involved in designing, developing, and deploying pages and apps. I excel at the critical thinking and problem-solving necessary to bring a digital project from concept to reality. I enjoy making products that combine functionality and aesthetics to provide users the best experience possible.

As a web developer, I keep a wide variety of skills and technologies in my toolbox. In addition to HTML/CSS/JavaScript (the bread and butter of the web), I am familiar with modern frameworks like Angular 2+ and React. I have worked with CMS like Wordpress, Hubspot, and Kentico. I have even dabbled in design tools like AdobeXD and GIMP. Ultimately, though, I consider my work less about the tools I use and more about the products I create. So I’m always interested in learning new languages, frameworks, and technologies to add to my repertoire.

Before working in web development, I taught ancient History at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, so I’m always up for a chat about the Romans. When not working, I enjoy cycling and hiking. Having lived in both the Rockies and Appalachia, I always enjoy a trip to the mountains!
